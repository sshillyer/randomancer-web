Ascension

High above the rising Mist, in the city of Drik'neth, four adventurers set out on a journey aboard the Dragon's Bane. Their mission: re-establish trade routes with the Spire Cities in the Southern Reaches.


Will they Rise Up, or will they Fall?

Impossible Heights

The Spire Cities of Ascencia defy all logic in their height and construction. Only through unimaginable magical techniques and ingenous engineering are these massive spires able to stand so high above the surface world.


Each spire is defined by its own economic, structural, and political characteristics. While one Spire may focus on slave labor and trading of humanoids as a mere commodity, another might raise its dead up to do the work that no living soul might be injured on the job.

Drik'Neth

The construction of the keep-like Spire City of Drik'Neth is completed by binding together massive stones in a magical process its Mechanics Guild refuses to share with any other spire. The massive stones used in construction are put in place by the multitude of slaves upon which the city's economy is built upon.
